About Us

I am orchard farmer and an ice-wine supplier. I supply several kinds of ice-wine to market. A customer, which can be a store, a super-market, or a person, makes an order for ice-wine by phone or just walking in. I fill in a card with their details, ice-wine requirement and calculate cost. I will deliver the ice-wine but it costs the more the farther away they are. I put the card in a box with other orders so I can find it easily when required.
